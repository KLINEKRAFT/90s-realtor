Welcome to my Home Page!

Hello, and thanks for stopping by! My name is Colin Kline, and for the past nine years I have had the privilege of helping families in the greater Tulsa area find the home of their dreams. Whether you are a first-time buyer, a growing family looking to move up, or an empty-nester ready to downsize, I would be honored to put my experience to work for you.

I am a proud Multi-Million Dollar Producer with Coldwell Banker Select, the largest real estate company in northeastern Oklahoma. From my office on East 71st Street, I serve buyers and sellers throughout South Tulsa, Midtown, Brookside, Jenks, Bixby, Broken Arrow, Owasso, and Sand Springs.

This is my brand-new World Wide Web site, and I am adding new properties and information every week. Be sure to bookmark this page — or, as we Netizens like to say, add it to your "Hot List"!
