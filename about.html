A Tulsan, Through & Through

I was born at Saint Francis Hospital in 1962 and have called Tulsa home for my entire life. I grew up in the Florence Park neighborhood, attended Eliot Elementary, Edison Preparatory School, and graduated from Oklahoma State University in 1984 with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (Finance concentration). Go Pokes!

After three years as a commercial loan officer at the Bank of Oklahoma, I realized that what I really enjoyed was the people side of the housing business — sitting at a kitchen table, walking through a backyard, helping a young couple imagine their future. In 1987 I earned my Oklahoma real estate license and joined Coldwell Banker. I have been here ever since.

Nine years and several hundred closings later, I still feel the same way about this job that I did on day one. Every transaction is somebody's biggest financial decision of the decade, and that deserves respect, patience, and straight talk. That is what I try to bring to the table, every single time.

My Philosophy

"A house is built of brick and beam.
A home is built of trust."

— Colin Kline

I believe that information is power, and that my job is to give you all of it — not just the good parts. If a roof has six years of life left in it, I will tell you. If the comps don't support the asking price, I will tell you. If your dream house has a foundation issue, I will tell you, even if it means walking away from a commission.

The Tulsa real estate community is small. My reputation is the only thing I really own, and I guard it carefully. Most of my business comes from past clients and their friends — which is exactly how I like it.

Professional Designations

Continuing education is a big part of what separates a good agent from an average one. Below are the designations I have earned over the years. Each one represents 40-90 hours of additional coursework beyond the standard real estate license.

GRI

Graduate, REALTOR® Institute. Earned 1990. The foundational designation of the National Association of REALTORS®; covers contract law, agency, finance, and ethics.

CRS

Certified Residential Specialist. Earned 1992. Held by fewer than 4% of REALTORS® nationwide. Requires documented production and advanced residential coursework.

ABR

Accredited Buyer's Representative. Earned 1994. The premier designation for agents who represent the buyer's side of the transaction.

SRES

Seniors Real Estate Specialist. Earned 1995. Specialized training in the housing needs of clients aged 50+, including reverse mortgages and downsizing strategy.

e-PRO Candidate

Currently enrolled in the NAR's pilot e-PRO program — learning how to use the World Wide Web and e-mail to better serve clients. (You are looking at one result of that training right now!)

Notary Public

Commissioned Oklahoma Notary Public since 1989. Saves clients a trip to the bank when paperwork needs witnessing.
